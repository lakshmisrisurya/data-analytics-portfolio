# Finance KPI & Collections Analytics

This project models accounts receivable and collections reporting using invoices and payments.

## Business Use Case
Finance and accounting teams need visibility into open balances, overdue exposure, and payment timelines to manage collections and cash flow effectively.

## Files
- `stg_invoices.sql`
- `stg_payments.sql`
- `fct_collections.sql`

## What This Demonstrates
- invoice and payment modeling
- outstanding balance calculation
- overdue logic
- collections performance reporting
