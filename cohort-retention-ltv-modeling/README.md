# Cohort Retention & LTV Modeling

This project demonstrates cohort-based customer retention and lifetime value modeling using recurring revenue and customer start dates.

## Business Use Case
Leadership and growth teams need to understand how customer cohorts perform over time, including retention patterns and long-term revenue contribution.
