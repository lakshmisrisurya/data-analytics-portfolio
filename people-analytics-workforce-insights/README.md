# People Analytics & Workforce Insights

This project demonstrates workforce analytics and hiring metrics used by HR, People Operations, and leadership teams.

## Business Use Case
People teams need visibility into workforce growth, attrition, tenure, hiring efficiency, and organization structure. This project models employee lifecycle and recruiting metrics for strategic planning.

## Metrics Included
- Active Headcount
- New Hires
- Terminations
- Attrition Rate
- Voluntary Attrition
- Involuntary Attrition
- Average Tenure
- Open Roles
- Time to Fill
- Time to Hire
- Offer Acceptance Rate
- Manager to Employee Ratio
- Span of Control

## Files
- `stg_employees.sql`
- `stg_requisitions.sql`
- `fct_headcount_attrition.sql`
- `fct_recruiting_efficiency.sql`

## What This Demonstrates
- employee lifecycle analytics
- workforce trend reporting
- hiring funnel analytics
- organizational health metrics
